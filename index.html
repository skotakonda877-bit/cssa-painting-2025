CSSSA Painting Intensive

August 4–8, 2025 · CalArts (Chouinard Hall)

Program Schedule Packing List FAQs Contact

Full Schedule

  • Day 1 (Aug 4): Orientation · Dorm check-in · Campus tour · Welcome mixer
  • Days 2–4 (Aug 5–7): Painting workshops · Critiques · Guest speakers · Evening studio time
  • Day 5 (Aug 8): Final presentations · Showcase · Closing ceremony · Dorm checkout

Packing List

  • Studio-safe clothes (x5 days)
  • Toiletries, towels, Twin XL bedding
  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Optional sketchbook/tools
  • Water bottle, sunscreen, chargers

Dorm Info

You’ll stay in Chouinard Hall, located on the CalArts campus. Rooms are double-occupancy with shared bathrooms. Bedding and towels not provided. Resident Assistants and CSSSA staff provide 24/7 supervision.

Program FAQs

  • Is this program free? Yes — tuition and housing are fully covered.
  • Are meals included? No, but campus dining and grocery options are available.
  • Who can attend? Students entering grades 9–12 or graduating high school in 2025.
  • Should I bring art supplies? Most supplies are provided, but personal sketchbooks/tools are welcome.
  • Questions? Email us at csssa.admissions@gmail.com
